Our Story

When they were young lads, Isaiah and Justin dreamed of driving and exploring the world. However, there was one major problem: wee little lads can't drive in the United States.

"Why can't I go to Tommy's birthday party, Mom?" Justin once asked.

"Because you can't drive, and I don't want to take you," she replied.

Those words stung, and Justin never forgot them. It wasn't until he met Isaiah and they both took ORF 401 at Princeton that everything started to make sense. A wise mentor advised them to create a business that would help people get where they wanted to go—whether or not they owned a car.

And so, Ryde was born.

At RYDE©, we value comfort, convenience, and experience. If you need a ride, we'll seamlessly and effortlessly get you where you need to go.
